How a cohort runs

Live, hands-on, and accountable.

01

Live sessions

2× weekly 90-minute workshops, recorded for time-zone flexibility. Taught by senior practitioners — never a "facilitator."

02

Weekly labs

Real infrastructure, real data, real failure modes. We give you a brief; you ship a working artifact by Friday.

03

Code reviews

Instructors review your PRs and leave line-level feedback. Same standards as our client engagements.

04

Capstone

Final two weeks: build a project you can show off, with a public write-up and demo day in front of hiring partners.

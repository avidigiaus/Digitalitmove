Enterprise Training Programs

On-site & Virtual

Technology investments only deliver value when your people have the skills to use them effectively. Digital IT Move provides comprehensive training programs that build lasting organizational capabilities. Our hands-on workshops, certification preparation courses, and executive education programs empower your teams to become self-sufficient in cybersecurity operations, cloud management, data analytics, and automation development. Training is delivered live (on-site or virtual) by practitioner-instructors who bring real-world experience.

Cybersecurity Training & Awareness Simulation-ready

Your employees are your first line of defense against cyber threats. Our security awareness training transforms your workforce from your greatest vulnerability to your strongest asset. Programs include: phishing simulation campaigns (sending simulated attacks, tracking click rates, providing just-in-time training for clickers), security fundamentals for all employees (password hygiene, multi-factor authentication, secure remote work, physical security), role-based training for executives (whale phishing awareness, regulatory obligations, incident reporting), IT teams (incident response procedures, security tool administration), and developers (secure coding practices, OWASP Top 10). Our incident response tabletop exercises simulate breach scenarios, testing your team's decision-making under pressure.

Azure Cloud Skills Development Certification Track

Building cloud capability requires more than documentation—it requires hands-on practice. We deliver Azure training aligned with Microsoft certification paths: AZ-900 (Azure Fundamentals) for executives and non-technical stakeholders covering cloud concepts, core services, pricing, and compliance; AZ-104 (Azure Administrator) for operations teams covering identity, governance, storage, compute, networking, monitoring, and backup; AZ-305 (Azure Solutions Architect) for architects designing solutions across infrastructure, data, security, and integration; and SC-200 (Security Operations Analyst) for security teams implementing threat protection and incident response.

Data Analytics & Power BI Workshops

Democratize data across your organization with our Power BI training. We offer workshops for different roles: business users learn to navigate dashboards, interpret visualizations, use Q&A natural language queries, and export data; analysts learn data modeling (relationships, calculated columns, measures using DAX), report design (visualizations, slicers, drill-through, bookmarks), and dashboard creation; power users learn advanced DAX (time intelligence, iterators, variables), row-level security, performance optimization, and Power BI Service administration.

Power Platform Citizen Development

Empower your business teams to solve their own problems with Power Platform. Our citizen development program teaches non-developers to build basic automations and applications safely and responsibly. Training covers: Power Automate fundamentals (creating automated approval flows, scheduled reports, button flows), Power Apps fundamentals (building simple canvas apps connected to SharePoint lists), responsible development practices (testing, documentation, error handling), and governance (data loss prevention policies, environment management, sharing guidelines).

Custom Technical Workshops

Need something specific? We design custom workshops addressing your exact technology stack and business challenges. Examples include: DevOps implementation workshop (assess current practices, design CI/CD pipeline, train team on GitHub Actions/Azure DevOps), data governance workshop (define data ownership, implement Microsoft Purview, train data stewards), secure development lifecycle workshop (integrate security scanning into development, train developers on threat modeling), and GRC automation workshop (use Power Platform to automate security questionnaires, risk assessments, compliance evidence collection).

Outcomes Guaranteed: Our training is practical, hands-on, and immediately applicable. Participants leave with skills they can use the next day.

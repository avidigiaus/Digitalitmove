Red Team Operations & Adversary Simulation

Our red team goes beyond standard penetration testing. We emulate real-world adversaries using MITRE ATT&CK framework to test your entire security ecosystem—people, processes, and technology. Our engagements include reconnaissance, weaponization, delivery, exploitation, installation, command & control, and actions on objectives. We simulate sophisticated attacks including ransomware campaigns, business email compromise, supply chain attacks, and insider threats. Every engagement is conducted under strict rules of engagement with full transparency and detailed reporting that includes actionable remediation roadmaps.

Proactive Threat Hunting

Traditional security monitoring waits for alerts. Our threat hunting service proactively searches through your telemetry to identify hidden adversaries before they strike. Using hypothesis-driven investigations based on threat intelligence, behavioral analytics, and anomaly detection, our hunters uncover threats that evade automated detection. We leverage EDR, SIEM, network logs, and cloud telemetry to identify patterns indicative of compromise—lateral movement, privilege escalation, data exfiltration, and persistence mechanisms.

Incident Response & Emergency Services

When a breach occurs, every minute counts. Our incident response team provides 24/7 emergency response with guaranteed response times. We follow the NIST incident response framework: preparation, detection & analysis, containment & eradication, recovery, and post-incident activity. Our containment strategies isolate affected systems while preserving evidence for legal proceedings. We work with your legal counsel and insurance providers to ensure proper chain of custody and regulatory notifications.

Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)

Our DFIR team combines incident response speed with deep forensic investigation capabilities. We conduct memory forensics, disk forensics, network forensics, and log analysis to determine root cause, impact assessment, and attacker tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs). Our forensic reports meet legal admissibility standards for litigation support, insurance claims, and regulatory compliance. We handle complex cases including ransomware attacks, data breaches, insider threats, and intellectual property theft.

Compliance & Governance

We help you navigate Australia's complex regulatory landscape with tailored compliance programs. Our services include gap assessments against Essential Eight, ISO 27001 implementation support, APRA CPS 234 compliance, Privacy Act data breach response plans, and customized security policies. All engagements are protected by strict NDAs and we ensure data sovereignty with all investigations conducted within Australian borders.

NDA & Compliance Guarantee: Every engagement begins with a mutual NDA. We never share client information, and all data remains within Australian sovereign boundaries.

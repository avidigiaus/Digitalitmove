How a build goes

From problem statement to production agent in 8 weeks.

Week 1–2 · Discovery & Eval Design

We co-author success criteria, build the offline eval set from your real historical data, and pick the smallest model that can clear the bar.

Week 3–4 · Architecture & Tooling

Pick the orchestration framework, design tool schemas, wire up retrieval. Everything lands in a repo with CI on day one.

Week 5–6 · Build & Tighten

Ship the agent, watch the traces, fix the failure modes. We usually find that 80% of the work is in the last 20% of polish.

Week 7 · Guardrails & HITL

Add the safety net: action allow-lists, PII redaction, escalation paths. Add the human checkpoints where the risk warrants them.

Week 8 · Production & Handoff

Deploy behind a feature flag. Train your team. Leave you with a runbook, an eval suite, and 30 days of on-call support.
